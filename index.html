Our Donation Plans

We have three plans in our donation system. Each plan ensures that it gets carried out by our officials effectively and immediately.

Food for a family

We propose to provide a monthly donation plan for a family in need, ensuring a consistent supply of nutritious food. This plan will include a variety of essential food items such as grains, proteins, fruits, and vegetables to meet their dietary requirements.

Shelter For a family

We present a comprehensive donation plan to provide shelter for a family in need. This plan includes funding for a safe and stable housing solution, covering rent and utilities for a specified period.

Education for a child

We propose a donation plan to support the education of a deserving child. This plan will cover the costs of tuition fees, school supplies, and educational resources.

Their lives depend on your Donation
